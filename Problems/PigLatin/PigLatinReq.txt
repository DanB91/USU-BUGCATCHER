1. PigLatin takes any number of words, and translates them into pig latin.
<br/>
2. If the word starts with a consonant or "ch," "th," or "sh," it moves the letter(s) to the end of the word and adds "-ay"
<br/>
3. If the word starts with a vowel or contains only one letter, it simply adds "-ay" to the end of the word.
<br/>
4. The output will always be in lowercase.
