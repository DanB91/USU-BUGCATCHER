1. This program runs as a simple calculator.
<br />
2. It accepts as input a simple math problem in the format "1 plus 1". It accepts plus, minus, over and times as operators.
<br />
3. The user can enter whole numbers "8 times 4" or numbers with decimals "8.4 over 2.2".
<br/>
4. Invalid inputs will output, "Error: Bad Input".
